NAVSITSUM

 

Message identifier (Name):

NAVSITSUM (NAVAL SITUATION SUMMARY)

 

 

Related Documents:

BI-SC REPORTING DIRECTIVE, VOL. III

 

 

Purpose:

THE NAVSITSUM IS USED TO PROVIDE A PERIODIC SUMMARY OF FRIENDLY FORCES GIVING DETAILS OF THEIR COMMAND, CONTROL, TASK ORGANIZATION, LOCATION AND PLANNED MOVEMENTS.

 

 

Sponsor:

BI-SC OPERATIONS REPORTS WORKING GROUP (ORWG).

 

 

Notes:

None

 

 

Remarks:

None

 

 

Status:

In Service
